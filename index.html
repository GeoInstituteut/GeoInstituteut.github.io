More About Us

The Research Institute for Development of Spatial Sciences, Technology, and Applications was established in 2016 as a collaboration between the University of Tehran and the Space Research Center, designated as a third-generation institution under the auspices of the Vice Presidency for Science and Technology of the Iranian government. In 2021, the University of Tehran appointed the Faculty of Geography to oversee this institute, with Dr. Najmeh Nisani Samani, an alumna of the University of Tehran with a degree in Surveying Engineering, and an associate professor at the Faculty of Geography, as the appointed head of this institute.
